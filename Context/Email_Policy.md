# Standard Recommendation
- Shorten emails to 50-75 words - shorter emails have higher response rates in cold outreach
- Differentiate follow-ups with progressive value - each email should introduce new information, angles, or proof points
- Use low-friction CTAs - try yes/no questions ("Worth a conversation?") or offer two specific times instead of open-ended requests
- Personalise with company name and first name where possible to increases relevance and response rates
- Address the recipient's pain points and value propositions - each email should address the recipient's pain points and value propositions
- Mention Recipient's name or Company's name at starting of the mail Subject and construct the rest of the subject accordingly.
