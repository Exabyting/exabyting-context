## Campaign Notes

- **Tone calibration for European market:** Professional but warm. Avoid aggressive American-style sales language. European executives respond better to consultative, peer-level communication.
- **GDPR compliance:** Ensure all contacts have a lawful basis for processing (legitimate interest for B2B outreach). Include an unsubscribe mechanism in your sending platform.
- **Timezone awareness:** Schedule sends between 08:00–10:00 local time for the prospect's timezone (CET/CEST for Central Europe, GMT/BST for UK/Ireland, EET for Eastern Europe).
- **Follow-up tracking:** Monitor open and reply rates per email. If Email 3 (custom software angle) outperforms Email 2 (staff augmentation angle), consider leading with the custom dev value prop in future campaigns.
